About Lamina Foundries

A legacy forged
in precision.

Lamina Foundries Limited, a public limited company of the Lamina Group, was commissioned in 1983 in Mangalore, Karnataka. Starting with cast iron castings for domestic customers — motor bodies, flywheels, valve bodies — we entered the export market in 1990, shipping precision-machined brake drums to Germany.

Award Karnataka State Export Performance 1996–2000
